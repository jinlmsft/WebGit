'''Jin Li''' is a [[computer scientist]]. He joined [[Microsoft Research]] in 1999, as one of the founding member of [[Microsoft Research Asia]]. He is currently a Partner Research Manger at Microsoft Research, Redmond
<ref NAME="jinlmsr">{{cite web | url=ttp://web.archive.org/web/20180723190042/https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/jinl/ | title=Jin Li, Partner Research Manager, Microsoft Research |work=Microsoft Research | access-date = August 14, 2018 }}</ref>. He has made prolific and significant contributions to multiple Microsoft products.  He demoed computer programs he has written to Deng Xiaoping in 1984, then the paramount leader of the People's Republic of China. This iconic event led to the quote “计算机普及要从娃娃” (Computer education should start from children), which was widely reported in China.

== Life and work ==
Li was born in [[Shanghai,China]].
In 1984, he demoed computer programs to Deng Xiaoping, then the paramount leader of the People's Republic of China. This iconic event led to the quote “计算机普及要从娃娃” (Computer education should start from children). This event and quote was widely reported in China,

In 1987, Li matriculated at [[Tsinghua University, Beijing, China]] at the age of 16. He got PhD in 1994. He was considered one of the youngest PhDs in China in the 20th century.
