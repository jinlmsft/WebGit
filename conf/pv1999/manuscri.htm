Manuscript Preparation      

1. HTML editors:

  • You may use the HTML editors bundled with several browsers (e.g., Netscape Navigator Gold, Netscape Communicator or MS Internet Explorer 3 and up, etc.) to type your manuscript in HTML directly.
  • MS Word 97 documents can be saved as HTML.
  • For converting from other formats, please refer to "HTML Converters" list kept by W3 organization.
  • To generate simple equations, you can use the "SUB" and "SUP" tags in HTML. For more complicated equations, you can generate a separate file for each equation using your favorite word processor, save the file in the postscript or GIF format, and then include the file as an in-line image.

2. Presentation of the final contribution:

  • You are encouraged to put all parts of your paper in a single document, except for non-text multimedia files that are accessed through hyper links. Please put all files that you may need for your submission in a single directory.
  • You may use "zip" or "tar" and "compress" for bundling and compressing them before sending the final submission to us.

3. Size constraints:

  • Your total submission should not exceed 7 MBytes.
  • Your HTML file, when printed, should not be longer than 10 pages.
  • Please make sure that your submission does not contain non-standard browser specific extensions. The reviewers are expected to have only one of the popular browsers.
  • It is your responsibility to provide any special software along with its usage instructions if such is needed for viewing any part of your submission.
